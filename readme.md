## Introduction

The Rogue Rebalancing mod is a gameplay modification for Baldur's Gate 2: Throne of Bhaal which strives to bring the Rogues (Bards and Thieves) of BG2 closer to their Pen and Paper AD&D counterparts. The mod is divided into multiple components all of which can be installed separately and are completely independent of each other.

*Please check the complete [Readme](https://htmlpreview.github.io/?https://github.com/FredrikLindgren/rr/blob/master/rr/doc/readme_rr.html) to learn more about this mod before installation.*

## Components

1. Proper dual-wielding implementation for Thieves and Bards
2. Thief kit revisions
3. Thief High Level Ability revisions
4. Proper racial adjustments for thieving skills
5. Bard Kit revisions
6. Bard High Level Ability revisions
7. Proper spell progression for Bards
8. Additional equipment for Thieves and Bards
9. Upgradeable equipment
10. Revised thievery
11. Chosen of Cyric encounter
12. Shadow Thief improvements
13. BG2-style icons for RR content
